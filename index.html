World Cleaners

A progression-based action simulator

Gameplay Details

Players begin their journey cleaning small areas filled with common waste. As they progress, dangerous corrupted zones unlock, introducing combat and rare collectible materials.

Progression System

Players begin their journey cleaning small areas filled with common waste. As they progress, new challenges are unlocked and progression features are introduced.

World & Enemies

As players clean deeper areas, they discover mutated creatures born from pollution. Each zone introduces new enemy mechanics and environmental storytelling.

About The Developers

The minds behind World Cleaners

Building Worlds Through Code

We are two passionate developers focused on creating immersive and progression-based experiences. Our main project, World Cleaners, combines action, strategy, and environmental storytelling inside the Roblox platform.

Rafael specializes in game systems, scripting, VFX maker, while Henrique specializaes on 3D modeling in Blender.
